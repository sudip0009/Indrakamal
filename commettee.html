About the Executive Committee

The Executive Committee is responsible for the overall management and governance of our cooperative. They make key decisions, set policies, and ensure that the organization operates in the best interests of its members.

This committee meets monthly to review financial performance, operational issues, and strategic initiatives. They work closely with other committees to ensure the cooperative's mission is fulfilled.

About the Advisory Committee

A three-member advisory committee consisting of individuals with experience in various fields provides essential guidance and contributions for the operation of the bus institution.

They meet quarterly to review our cooperative's performance, assess market conditions, and provide recommendations to the Executive Committee.

About the Loan Committee

The Loan Committee is responsible for reviewing and approving loan applications from our members. They ensure that all loans meet our cooperative's lending criteria and that members receive fair and transparent financing options.

The committee meets every Tuesday and Thursday to review applications and make decisions. They follow strict guidelines to maintain the financial health of our cooperative while supporting members' financial needs.
