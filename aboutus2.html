Who We Are

Gndrakamal is a member-owned cooperative dedicated to providing inclusive and sustainable services to our community.

Our Cooperative Story

"Indrakamal Multipurpose Co-operative Limited" is an organization granted approval to conduct financial transactions under the Co-operative Act 2076, with permission from the Provincial Government. The organization’s principal office is located at Kathmandu-1, Durbarmarg. The management level of this organization consists of experienced and successful individuals who have been active in the financial sector for a long time. The organization's main objective is to strengthen its current financial transactions securely, value the hard work of the community, and integrate small capital. It aims to increase investments and boost profit according to its objective by playing an active and functional role in the business sector, agricultural sector, social service sector, and other sectors. This is done to improve the economic standard by providing concessional loans to members, create an entrepreneurial and skilled-based society, and move forward impartially while respecting the value, recognition, development, and law of a multipurpose co-operative in the process of national economic development with fairness and selflessness

Our Workflow

How we serve our members step by step

Step 1: Membership

Individuals join our cooperative by becoming members and opening a savings account.

Step 2: Regular Savings

Members save regularly, building financial discipline and strengthening our community fund.

Step 3: Credit Facilities

We provide affordable loans to members for personal, business, or emergency needs.

Step 4: Support & Guidance

Our cooperative offers financial literacy, guidance, and support to help members succeed.

Step 5: Community Growth

Through unity, savings, and cooperation, we aim for collective prosperity and community development.

Our Cooperative Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Voluntary Membership

Open to all without gender, social, racial, political or religious discrimination.

Democratic Control

One member, one vote - regardless of how much money a member has invested.

Member Participation

Members actively participate in setting policies and making decisions.

Autonomy & Independence

We maintain our democratic member control while working with other organizations.
